FOREWORD

Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics and Finance (UEF), Agency for the Southern Affairs of Ministry of Science and Technology and Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HUTECH) were honored to host the 2nd annual International Conference on Science, Economics and Society Studies (ICSES 2023). The conference was held in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam on August 25, 2023. The purpose of this conference series is to provide a forum for communication between researchers and organizations. It is gratifying that there are 94 papers published in the proceedings.

My gratitude also extends to the special contribution of the professors and researchers from partner universities and institutions.
