FOREWORD

Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics and Finance (UEF), Agency for the Southern Affairs of Ministry of Science and Technology and Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HUTECH) were honored to host the 2nd annual International Conference on Science, Economics and Society Studies (ICSES 2023). The conference was held in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam on August 23, 2023. The purpose of this conference series is to provide a forum for communication between researchers and organizations. It is gratifying that there are 94 papers published in the proceedings.

We are pleased to acknowledge the official sponsorship of the conference by Sacombank and VNSTELL who have made generous support and contributed to the success of the conference.

In addition, the success of ICSES 2023 is not possible without the concerted effort of the organizers, reviewers and all the participants. On behalf of the organizing committee, I would like to thank all the authors for their contribution of paper to the conference. My gratitude also extends to the special contribution of the professors and researchers from partner universities and institutions.

We hope that this program will encourage further stimulate research in Science, Economics and Society Studies, and provide practitioners with useful techniques, knowledge, and tools for advancement in their fields. We feel honored and privileged to create a forum to share and exchange ideas through this exciting program.

Best regards,

Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
Program Committee Co-Chair
Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ngo Cao Cuong
Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics and Finance
